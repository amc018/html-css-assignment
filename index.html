Atomic Habits By James Clear

This book gave me a science backed way of how to cultivate good habits and get rid of bad ones.

Deep Work by Cal Newport

This book opened my eyes to the urgency of deep undistracted work to thrive in this distracted world.

Mindset: The New Psychology Of Success by Carol Dweck

Prior to reading this book, I had a fixed mindset. This book was an eyeopener for me.

The Holy Bible

My daily pill, it revives me everyday and helps me navigate through my difficulties.
